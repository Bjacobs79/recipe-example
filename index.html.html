The perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich.It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of jelly
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • a butter knife
  • A cutting board

    Directions

  1. lay both slices of bread next to eachother
  2. spread peanut butter on the left slice of bread
  3. spread jelly on right side of bread
  4. carefully put the two halfs of bread together
